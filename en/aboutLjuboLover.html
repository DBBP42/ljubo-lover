Our Mission

About Ljubo-Lover

Ljubo-Lover.com is a trauma-informed platform dedicated to providing clear, accessible, and compassionate information for survivors of sexual abuse, their families, and the professionals who support them.

Why We Exist

Breaking the silence

Information as Power

We believe that understanding the legal system, the psychology of trauma, and your rights is a critical part of healing.

Trauma-Informed Design

Our platform is designed to be safe, non-triggering, and easy to navigate, even in moments of high stress.

Zero JS, Pure Privacy

We use no JavaScript and no tracking. Your privacy and safety are our highest technical priorities.

Global Reach

While we started in Slovenia, our mission is to provide high-quality information to survivors across Europe and the world.

Our Values

What we stand for

  • Belief: We start from a place of believing survivors.
  • Dignity: Every person deserves to be treated with respect, regardless of their past or identity.
  • Transparency: We are clear about the limitations of the system and the reality of the path to healing.
  • Independence: We are not affiliated with any government or religious organization.

You are the reason we are here

If this platform helps even one person feel less alone or more empowered to seek help, we have achieved our goal. Thank you for being here.
